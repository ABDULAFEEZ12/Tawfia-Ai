About Tawfiq AI

Tawfiq AI is the world’s first halal AI assistant, designed by Tella Abdul Afeez Adewale.

It helps Muslims learn, practice, and live Islam in the digital age by combining authentic sources such as the Quran and Hadith with modern AI-powered tools.

Our mission is to make Islamic knowledge accessible, engaging, and practical for Muslims everywhere — always within halal boundaries.

We believe in creating technology that serves both faith and modern needs, making Tawfiq AI a trusted companion in your spiritual journey.

Feel free to contact us for more information or feedback.
